Research Interests & Projects

My research spans multiple disciplines including wireless communications, statistical analysis, machine learning, and data processing. I focus on practical applications that can solve real-world problems and advance our understanding of complex systems.

Big Data Transmission & Wireless Networks

Primary research focus on Big Data transfer through multibeam antennae, involving matrix decompositions, data preservation, multibeam communication and graph theory.

Key Areas:

  • Queuing models for high-throughput wireless networks
  • Multi-hop data transmission optimization
  • Network capacity planning and analysis
  • Router queue management algorithms

Current Work: Developing optimal solutions for data transfer over multiple hops in wireless networks with massive data transfer capabilities.

Statistical Methods & Matrix Decomposition

Advanced statistical analysis techniques including PCA, ICA, and related factor analysis methods for various applications.

Research Focus:

  • Perturbation properties of factor analysis
  • QR decomposition vs SVD comparison studies
  • Algorithm optimization for large-scale data
  • Financial modeling applications

Notable Finding: S&P market analysis shows no single factor can provide reliable one-day-ahead prediction capabilities.

Image Retrieval & Machine Learning

Feature extraction and analysis for improved image recognition and retrieval systems.

Technical Approach:

  • Scale Invariant Feature Transform (SIFT) optimization
  • Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) applications
  • Independent Component Analysis (ICA) for feature analysis
  • Feature redundancy identification and elimination

Applications: Bandwidth-constrained information retrieval systems and computer vision applications.

Computational Optimization & AI

Development of efficient algorithms for communication-constrained environments and AI applications.

Current Projects:

  • Optimal Load Sharing (OLS) algorithms
  • Communication-constrained computation environments
  • Deep learning model optimization
  • Cybersecurity applications in AI

Industry Collaboration: Voluntary consultant work on deep learning models with EH Group Inc. (2022-2023).
