Current Research Interests

Factor Analysis & Statistical Methods

Research into perturbation properties of factor analysis, PCA, ICA, and related statistical analysis techniques for better algorithms and approaches.

Matrix QR Decomposition

Exploring the power and elegance of QR decomposition as a faster alternative to SVD with orders of magnitude less complexity.

Image Retrieval & ML

Feature extraction from images using SIFT, analyzing redundancy through NMF and ICA for improved image recognition systems.

Big Data Transmission

Developing queuing models for optimal data transfer over multiple hops in high-throughput wireless networks with massive data capabilities.
